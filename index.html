Smart Expenses Plan

Smart Expenses Plan is a dedicated financial management application designed to help users track spending and maintain budgets. Our app allows you to securely backup your expense data to your personal Google Drive and restore it whenever you switch devices.

Application Purpose

The primary goal of Smart Expenses Plan is to provide users with a private, secure way to manage their finances. By using Google Drive for backups, we ensure that your sensitive financial data stays under your control in your own cloud storage.

View our Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Smart Expenses Plan ("the App") respects your privacy. We only access the "Google Drive App Data" folder to save and load your backup files.

We do not share your data with third parties. All data processing happens locally on your device or within your personal Google account.
