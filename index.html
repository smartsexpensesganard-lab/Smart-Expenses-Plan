Manage Your Finances with Smart Expenses Plan

Smart Expenses Plan is a comprehensive financial tool designed to help users track daily spending, set monthly budgets, and visualize their savings goals. By connecting to your Google account, the app securely syncs your budget data across devices so you never lose track of your financial health.

App Purpose

Our mission is to simplify personal accounting through automated data syncing and intuitive expense categorization.
